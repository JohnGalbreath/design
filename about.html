Hello!

My name is Johnathan, I’m a USAF Veteran currently going through the Graphic Design program at Portland State University pursuing my Bachelor’s Degree in Fine Arts.

Towards the end of 2019 when I was getting ready to leave the Air Force, I started livestreaming on Twitch.com and discovered my desire to create designs that others and I could use in the digital space of content creation. I later started developing these skills and applying them to animation design for channels on Twitch and YouTube to use in their videos. While pursuing my degree at PSU I have completed various passion projects as well as a few one-off projects such as department designs, shirts, and patches for clients at Legacy Health System. No matter if it’s for a personal project of mine, or contractual work for clients, I’m always happy to delve into the creative process in any format.

Any questions about the process of commissioning work can be found in the FAQ page. To start the commissioning process feel free to check out the Contact page.
